Booking

To book a package, please use the contact us page to email us the following:

  • Desired package if applicable
  • The type of photoshoot you need – family/portrait/pet/home
  • Date and location of your photoshoot
  • Number of people in the session
  • Any special requirements

We’ll get back to you with our availability and a personalised quote.

Payment


Once a date, location and cost of your photoshoot are agreed, you’ll be sent further information on the payment. We ask for a 10% non-refundable deposit to be paid in advance of your photoshoot to secure the date, the rest of the payment will be due once your photos are delivered. You’ll be asked to sign a contract which will outline the responsibilities for the photographer and yourself. To make a payment, please use your cart to add a custom amount.




Preparing for your photoshoot

  • Pick out your clothes early, some guidance will be sent once you’ve booked a photoshoot
  • Practice your hair and make-up, take some photos on your phone to see if you like this look. Please email us if you need hair and make-up assistance
  • Avoid the sun or wear a sunscreen for a few days before your photoshoot
  • Drink lots of water the day before and get plenty of rest
  • Use a moisturising cream on your face and body on the day of the photoshoot
  • Bring any props you’d like to use (please discuss these with us prior to your photoshoot)
